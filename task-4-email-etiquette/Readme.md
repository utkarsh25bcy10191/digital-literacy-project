Task 4: Professional Email & Etiquette Guide

This module focuses on the art of professional digital communication. As a Student Digital Ambassador, I have developed resources to help peers navigate formal email writing and maintain a responsible social media presence.

Folder Contents

emails.txt: Contains two formal email drafts (Extension Request & Internship Inquiry).

social-media-checklist.md: A comprehensive 10-point guide for safe and professional social media use.

Part A: Professional Email Drafts I have drafted two essential emails following standard professional protocols:

1. Assignment Extension Request: Focuses on a polite tone, clear subject line, and providing a specific new deadline.
2. Internship Inquiry: Demonstrates how to introduce oneself, state intent clearly, and provide professional links (like LinkedIn).

Key Etiquette Followed:

-Professional salutations and sign-offs.

-Concise and purpose-driven body text.

-Clear, searchable subject lines.

Part B: Social Media Responsibility The Online Media Checklist included in this folder serves as a safety manual for college students. It covers:

-Privacy: Protecting sensitive data and location.

-Security: The importance of 2FA and unique passwords.

-Reputation: Managing a "Digital Footprint" that appeals to future employers.

Reflection Summary In this task, I explored how small mistakes in digital communication—like using informal slang in a formal email—can lead to missed opportunities. Practicing these drafts has helped me transition from a "student" mindset to a "professional" one.
