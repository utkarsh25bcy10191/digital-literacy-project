digital-literacy-project
Overview

The Digital Literacy Project is a comprehensive educational initiative aimed at enhancing awareness, skills, and responsible usage of digital technologies. This project covers essential topics like digital platforms, email etiquette, cybercrime awareness, and creating portfolios, providing a structured approach for students and beginners to understand the digital landscape effectively.

This repository contains all project tasks, assignments, presentations, and related resources developed as part of the course curriculum for VIT CSE – CYBERSECURITY & DIGITAL FORENSICS, Batch 2029.

Project Structure

The repository is organized as follows: digital-literacy-project/ │ ├── Task-1-Presentation/ # Slides and documents for Task 1 ├── Task-2-Portfolio/ # Portfolio assignment submissions ├── Task-3-Platforms/ # Exploration of digital platforms ├── Task-4-Email-Etiquette/ # Guidelines for professional email usage ├── Task-5-Cybercrime/ # Awareness and study of cybercrime ├── README.md # Project overview and instructions └── assets/ # Images, graphics, or reference materials

Tasks Overview

Task 1 – Presentation

Topic: Introduction to Digital Literacy Includes infographics, canva Objective: Understand the importance and scope of digital literacy

Task 2 – Portfolio

Topic: Personal digital portfolio Includes digital portfolios like Github, Kaggle , Linkedin Objective: Showcase digital skills professionally

Task 3 – Digital Platforms

Topic: Overview of commonly used digital platform Hackerrank and goggle forms. Objective: Learn about platform functionalities, advantages, and safe usage

Task 4 – Email Etiquette

Topic: Best practices for professional email communication Objective: Develop effective and polite digital communication skills

Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness

Topic: Understanding cyber threats and preventive measures Objective: Educate about online safety, common cybercrimes, and legal implications
